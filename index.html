Welcome to the privacy policy for Friend Zone on Android.

The application Friend Zone (hereinafter called "The App") does not store or transmit any data remotely.

Information about contacts and their associated timezones is stored in the Android main system preferences to ensure that the widgets stays the same following a phone restart. The App will access contact information and the main contact profile, to ensure your widget has a lovely photograph to show of your friends.
